Sunday M.I.A.

Each week a handful of players are projected to go off — and almost none of them play in your market. The eight highest-projected starters are scattered across out-of-market games your local channels won't carry. Sunday M.I.A. puts all eight in one ad: a different CTV spot for every city, every day — and only Sunday Ticket can resolve it.
