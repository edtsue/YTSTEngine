The recap First time ever

Three firsts, one campaign.

Set Your Sunday stacks innovations that have never run together in a CTV ad before.

01

CTV contextual creative

One ad recomposes for every day of the fantasy week — meeting each manager at the decision they're making, from waivers to lineup lock. First time a CTV spot is rebuilt around the planning cycle, not a fixed flight.

02

Genius Sports player performance data

The Elite Eight are cast by Genius Sports performance projections — who's about to go off this week, not who went off last week. First time forward-looking data drives who's in the ad and who you're told to start.

03

AI-Assembly

Backdrop, headline and chrome are generated and assembled on the fly — a finished, rights-clean spot in seconds. First time the creative is built by the engine, not the edit bay.

Set Your Sunday — a contextual creative engine concept for YouTube Sunday Ticket. Players, logos and brand marks shown are illustrative for pitch purposes; the live engine pulls the real Elite Eight top-projected players each week — modeled on Genius Sports performance data. Every spot features the Elite Eight together — a group feature, never a solo endorsement.
